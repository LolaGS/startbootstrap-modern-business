Book Recommendations for Bookworms

This is a website for great books that I have read!

The Percy Jackson Series by Rick Riordan

>

Percy Jackson is what got me interested in reading! I highly recommend the entire series. The story centers around the life of a 12 year old boy named Percy Jackson. He finds out that is the son of Posiden - the Greek god of oceans. Together, he and his friends (who are also half Greek god) go on many adventures to save the world. I love Annabeth Chase because she reminds me a lot of myself. Who was your favorite character?

The Harry Potter Series by JK Rowling

>

Harry Potter is a classic. If you love magic, England, and adventure, this book is for you! I'm sure that you've heard of this series. It is quite popular! It is also one of the rare cases where the movie is just as good as the book! It follows the story of an 11 year old boy named Harry Potter who learns that he is a wizard and is being hunted by Lord Voldemort, and incredibly evil dark lord who wants to kill Harry! I'm definitely a Slytherin, what house do you belong in?

The Darkest Minds by Alexandra Bracken

>

This book is a little darker than my first two recommendations, but one of my favorites. It has everything! Magic, adventure, romance, and a road trip! The story is about a girl named Ruby who lives in a post apocalyptic world where a mutation that affects only children give kids super natural powers. Ruby is an "orange," which means that she has the most powerful power. What would be your PSI power?

The Mortal Instruments by Cassandra Clare

>

These books are amazing! They are about a group of teenage shadow hunters - half human half angel deities - that have to save the world from the evil demon race. I love these books and there are a ton of spin offs so you don't have to say good bye to the characters! My favorite character is Clary, what about you?

A Court of Thorns and Roses by Sarah J Maas

>

This is a new favorite! ACOTAR centers around a human girl named Feyre who finds herself thrust into a world of fae. This is a more mature novel, so make sure that your ready for some harder concepts and subjects. What was your favorite Court? I love the Night Court but thats mainly because of the characters that live there.

Anne of Green Gables by Lucy Maude Montgomery

>

Of course, I needed to include a classic. Anne of Green Gables tells a beautiful story about an orphan girl who is adopted by a couple in Canada. The story takes place in the late 1800's, so the authors description of the world is quite different from ours. If you like adventure, the magic that comes with a strong imagination, and the reality of our past worlds, this story is definitely for you.
